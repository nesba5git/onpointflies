Our Story

Born on the Water, Built at the Vise

On Point Flies started with a simple passion: tying flies that work. What began at a tying vise in a garage has grown into a catalog of hundreds of proven patterns, each one refined through countless hours on the water.

We believe that a well-tied fly is the connection between the angler and the fish. That's why we put craftsmanship first in everything we do.
