The Beginning

A Passion That Started on the River

Fly fishing is more than a hobby -- it's a way of life. On Point Flies was born from a deep appreciation for the art of fly tying and a desire to share quality, hand-crafted flies with anglers everywhere.

What started at a small tying vise, experimenting with materials and testing patterns on local rivers, has grown into a catalog of proven fly patterns. Every fly we tie carries the same passion and attention to detail that sparked this journey.

Whether you're chasing trout in mountain streams, targeting bass in warm water, or drifting nymphs through riffles, we have patterns designed to produce results.

The Sport

The Art of Fly Fishing

Fly fishing is a method of angling where an artificial "fly" is used to catch fish. The fly is cast using a fly rod, reel, and specialized weighted line. It's popular for trout, salmon, and many other species in rivers and streams worldwide.

Unlike conventional fishing, fly fishing relies on the weight of the line rather than the lure to deliver the fly. This creates a unique, graceful casting motion that has captivated anglers for centuries.

Whether you're a beginner discovering the sport or an experienced angler refining your technique, the right fly makes all the difference. That's where we come in.
