The Art of Fly Tying

On Point Flies was born from a simple passion: creating flies that catch fish. What started at a small tying bench has grown into a dedication to producing high-quality, effective patterns.

Every fly is hand-tied with premium materials, ensuring durability and fish-catching action. We test our patterns extensively on local waters.
