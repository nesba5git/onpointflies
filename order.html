Getting Started

How to Order

Ordering from On Point Flies is simple. Follow these steps to get your fly box stocked.

Browse the Catalog

Check out our Fly Catalog and Inventory to see available patterns and stock levels.

Contact Us

Use the contact form or email info@onpointflies.com with your selections, quantities, and any custom requests.

Custom Details

For custom flies, share a description, photo, reference image, or details from a past order. We'll discuss the design with you.

Confirm & Pay

We'll send a quote and invoice. Pay via PayPal. A deposit may be required for custom orders.

We Tie & Ship

Once payment is received, we tie your flies with care and ship them to your door.
