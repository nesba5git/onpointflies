Order Your Fly Fishing Flies

At On Point Flies, we offer a selection of high-quality, hand-tied flies from our catalog, as well as custom orders tailored to your needs. Whether you're restocking classics or designing something unique, we're here to help. All orders are processed manually—contact us to get started!

How to Order

  1. Browse the Catalog: Check out our Fly Catalog for available patterns.
  2. Contact Us: Use the contact form or email info@onpointflies.com with your selections, quantities, and any custom requests.
  3. Provide Details for Customs: For custom flies, share a description, photo URL, uploaded image, or reference to a past order. We can discuss designs via email or phone. [](grok_render_citation_card_json={"cardIds":["2b8588","b49953","50a071"]})
  4. Confirmation & Payment: We'll send a quote and invoice. Pay via PayPal, Venmo, or other methods. A non-refundable deposit may be required for customs. [](grok_render_citation_card_json={"cardIds":["607899","013fee"]})
  5. Production & Shipping: Once paid, we'll tie and ship your order.

We ship worldwide via USPS, DHL, or similar carriers. Shipping costs: $5-15 domestic, $20+ international (calculated at checkout or in quote). [](grok_render_citation_card_json={"cardIds":["5676a2","9245db"]})
